The Leadey API gives you programmatic access to the lead infrastructure behind the product — the same engine that sources accounts, enriches contacts, detects buying signals, and runs multi-channel outreach across LinkedIn, email, and cold calling.

Use it to pull qualified leads into your own systems, react to signals as they fire, and launch outreach from your own workflows — without leaving your stack.

</Tip>
